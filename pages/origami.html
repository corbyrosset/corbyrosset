Philosophy on Grades: each person is random variable as well as each grade. Thus a single test is hardly an indication of any one person's understanding. And three exams per class is a very poor random sample. But by the law of large numbers, many people working cooperatively will consistently perform better than a single person. This is why companies and governments exist. Furthermore, emergent properties of complex interactions between people lead to "best of many worlds" innovation
